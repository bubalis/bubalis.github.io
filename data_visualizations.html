Data Visualizations

A Couple of Data-Viz Projects I am Proud of:

Some of these relate to my work, but most of them are hobbyist side-projects.



  • Interactive Map of the 2021 City of Burlington Reappraisal

    • This is not the prettiest visualization, but I made this in a few hours responding to a need in my community. The local newspaper took about two weeks longer to produce an interactive visualization of this *highly contentious* local issue.



  • DIY UVM COVID-19 Dashboard

    • I don't maintain this anymore but when it was active, it was the only dashboard for COVID at the University of Vermont.



  • Vermont Covid Cases by County

    • Also no longer updated, but I'm quite proud of this spatial visualization.



  • Modelled Runoff from Tropical Storm Irene

    • Results of using the NRCS curve-number method to understand runoff produced by Hurricane Irene.



  • Map of Every Organic Farm in the US
